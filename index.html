How do i buy a template?

To buy a template you have to click on the bag icon below the template after clicking you'll be redirected to another URL on buymeacoffee.com where you have to make the payment or if the chosen template is free of cost then the download button of code files will be available just below the template

How do i get the template's code files?

While making the payment at buymeacoffee.com you are asked for your email id, After the payment is done the code files will be mailed to that email within the next 24 hours of making the payment.

You should keep in mind that..

Before you make any kind of transaction on our site, there are a few things that you should keep in mind. We work on NO REFUND POLICY. That means no matter what the condition is, if you have made the payment, you are not going to get your money back. If money has been deducted from your card but we didn't get it, you must take a screenshot and mail it to us at shekharshashank605@gmail.com. Our team will look at it and you will be provided with the code files of that template.
